About Me

Syed Muzzamil Ali is a postgraduate student from Hyderabad, Telangana, currently pursuing an M.Tech in Computer Science (AI & ML) from Osmania University. With a background in computer science and a growing interest in new technologies, he has developed skills in Java, Python, and web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Additionally, he is familiar with databases such as MySQL, MariaDB, and Firestore. His academic journey has been complemented by hands-on experience in AI and machine learning projects, particularly in sentiment analysis, where he worked on a real-time multilingual sentiment analysis system using Google Cloud. His work involved collecting data from various platforms via APIs and processing it in real-time using tools like Google Pub/Sub and Google Dataflow.

During his B.Tech at Bharat Institute of Engineering and Technology, Muzzamil worked on AI and machine learning applications to solve real-world problems. His mini project, "Missing Child Identification System Using Deep Learning" (2021), focused on identifying missing children by comparing registered photos in a repository using deep learning techniques. For his major project, "Network Intrusion Detection System Using Machine Learning" (2022), he implemented various machine learning models to detect and classify network intrusions. Currently, he is dedicated to enhancing his technical expertise and gaining practical experience. He is actively seeking opportunities to apply his skills, explore new technologies, and contribute to impactful projects.
